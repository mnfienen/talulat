Got back from my trip - which was a good one! But since getting back been crushed by a cough and cold. But nonetheless we managed to try out the new fron panniers, pack up the bike, clear out work things, and get ready to go!

Bike is in pieces and in boxes, we laid out all the gear, clothes, and everything we need to bring. It's constantly getting more real. The weather is bonkers though - we had to bail on my dad's birthday in Minnesota this weekend due to a massive blizzard rolling in. It was a huge bummer to miss, but it did give us a quiet weekend to calmly reassess the trip beta and gear and carefully pack things up. We finalized some plans for the end of the trip in Obihiro, changed a couple route/accomodations things, and basically getting to the point where whatever we day, we go with what we know and what we have.

I think weather is going to remain a theme! Sterling sent us a video of snow in Tokyo but also told us if there are two snows in Tokyo in March (this was the second!) then the cherry blossoms are imminent. Then he sent this photo.

<img src="../../../assets/images/day-2/さくら.jpg" width=450>  

So at least in Tokyo, spring is happening! 

We are taking some chances with weather on this one, but we are hopeful! But on the topic of weather, the blizzard here is ramping up - Minnesota got slammed! We got options to change our flights, but no options worked out, so hopefully in 36 hours we are on our way!

We probably won't blog too much in Tokyo while visiting family, but the riding starts on March 27. Then it's on!!!
